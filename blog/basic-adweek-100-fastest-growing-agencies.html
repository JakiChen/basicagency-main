BASIC® Deemed One of the World’s Fastest Growing Agencies by Adweek
(09.25.19)

Adweek named BASIC® as part of its first annual global ranking of the Fastest Growing Agencies. Ranked among agencies across 21 disciplines and categories, our shop secured the 18th overall spot on the list and came in 8th for midsize agencies as well as 4th in the West.

As a digital product and experience agency, BASIC® has recently built a reputation positioning brands to matter in culture. With a recent string of globally renowned clients in fashion, luxury, hospitality, and fintech, we grew 355% throughout a three-year span and more prominently within the past year and a half.

This is a huge honor, and I'm extremely proud of everything we're doing together.
Matt Faulk
CEO at BASIC®

Agencies were asked to report three years of earned revenue between 2016 and 2018. According to Adweek, “ . . . 85 of the top 100 sat between $10 million and $50 million, and 23 had revenue of over $50 million, with several clocking in at nine figures.

As Adweek puts it so aptly, growth is a result of the “ . . . talented, dedicated individuals and teams that ignore the noise around them to focus on what they do best: delivering big time for their clients and brands.”
